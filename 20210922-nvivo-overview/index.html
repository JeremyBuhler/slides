Why use NVivo?

In the data analysis process you're looking for relationships, identifying themes, exploring patterns in your source material, sometimes working with material collected in different ways and indifferent formats. NVivo is designed around the processes involved in doing this kind of work.

It gives you many ways of taking notes and recording your comments and notes as you're working, linking those notes so they're easier to find later. Stress the importance of recording notes about your process, of telling the story of your project, of tracking how your observations and thinking evolves as you work.

And it keeps everying in one place - your NVivo project is just one file, but that single file can contain your source material, your observations, any work you might to to code the text for themes - ideally in a way that's easier to keep track of.

  • Designed around the QDA process
  • Keep source material and notes in one place
  • Code and query to find patterns and relationships
